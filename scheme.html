SCHEMES by GOVT OF INDIA

  • Vigyan Jyoti Scheme: Launched by the Department of Science & Technology, this program encourages meritorious girls from Class 9 to 12 to pursue higher education and careers in STEM. It offers science camps, special lectures, counseling, and interactions with role models to motivate girls.
  • Women in Science and Engineering-KIRAN (WISE-KIRAN): This initiative aims to enhance women's participation in science and technology by providing fellowships for Ph.D. programs, research grants, and support for women scientists.
  • Skill India Mission: Launched in 2015, Skill India aims to train over 30 crore people in various skills by 2022, with a focus on empowering women through skill development programs.
  • Namo Drone Didi Scheme: Introduced in November 2023, this scheme empowers rural women by providing them with agricultural drones and training them to become drone pilots, enhancing their participation in modern agriculture.
  • STEM for Girls Program: An initiative by the American India Foundation, this program aims to increase the number of girls pursuing STEM education by providing mentorship, resources, and exposure to STEM careers.
  • GATI (Gender Advancement for Transforming Institutions): Launched by the Department of Science & Technology, GATI is a pilot program to promote gender equity in STEM institutions through self-assessment and action plans.
  • Udaan Scheme: Aimed at providing quality education to young girls from Jammu and Kashmir, Udaan offers free coaching for entrance exams to premier engineering and medical colleges.
  • National Policy for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship: This policy focuses on empowering women through skill development programs, aiming to increase their participation in the workforce, including in STEM fields.
  • Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY): A flagship scheme to provide industry-relevant skill training to youth, with a significant focus on women, to enhance their employability in various sectors, including STEM.
